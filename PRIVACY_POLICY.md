# Privacy Policy for SplitBill

**Last Updated: June 20, 2026**

SplitBill ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our SplitBill mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide you with expense-sharing and group bill-splitting features, we collect the following information:

- **Account Information**: Your email address, display name, and profile picture (optional) when you register an account.
- **Verification Data**: One-Time Passwords (OTPs) sent to your email to verify your identity.
- **Group & Expense Data**: Names of groups you create or join, expense amounts, categories (e.g., groceries, rent), transaction descriptions, dates, and split balances between you and other group members.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate your account and keep you securely logged in.
- Enable you to create, join, and manage expense groups.
- Calculate and split balances accurately between group members.
- Display transaction and activity histories on your group feed.
- Send transactional emails (like OTP verification) to secure your account.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- **Database**: All information is stored in a secure MongoDB database.
- **Security Protocols**: Passwords are encrypted using modern hashing algorithms (bcryptjs), and communication between the app and the backend server is secured using JSON Web Tokens (JWT).
- **Data Sharing**: We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers. Data is only shared among the members of the groups you explicitly choose to join.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services for essential backend operations:
- **Resend API / Nodemailer**: To securely send transactional OTP emails.
- **Cloudinary**: To store profile/avatar images.
